### MITRE Attack
https://attack.mitre.org/tactics/enterprise/
A standardization for how attacks are normally executed. It's an attack framework.

- Used for interviews
- Different threat actors and groups are enumerated

### Forensics
The investigatory process of identifying how a crime took place.

- traditionally based on physical evidence

### Digital Forensics

Digital forensics arose as a response to cyber crime. Digitial Forensics if a form of abductive reasoning using a set of evidence to justify an outcome. This makes forensics as a whole a scientific endeavor.

**Seven Forensic Principles**
- Law of Individuality
- Principle of Exchange
- Law of Progressive Change
- Law of Comparison
- Law of Analysis
- Law of Probability
- Law of Circumstantial Facts

**Process of Digital Forensics**

5. Analysis
6. Present
